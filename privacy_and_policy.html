Introduction
Who we are and why this policy matters

WakaSafe is a personal safety and journey-monitoring application developed by Voddic LTD, a technology company based in Nigeria. WakaSafe is designed to help users keep trusted contacts informed during travel and emergency situations — operating primarily through offline SMS-based GPS location sharing.

This Privacy Policy explains in clear terms what personal information we collect from you when you use the WakaSafe application, how that information is used, stored, protected, and shared. It also describes your rights with respect to your personal data.

🛡️
Your Consent
By downloading, installing, registering for, or using WakaSafe, you confirm that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy and agree to the collection and use of your information as described herein. If you do not agree, please do not use the application.

This policy applies to the WakaSafe Android application and all associated services operated by Voddic LTD. It does not apply to third-party services integrated into WakaSafe, such as Paystack, which have their own separate privacy policies.

Background Location Usage
When and why WakaSafe accesses location in the background

WakaSafe requests background location access solely to support the core safety function of the application. Background location is a critical feature for traveler safety in areas with unreliable network conditions — it ensures your contacts continue to receive updates even if your phone screen is off or you are unable to interact with the app.

📍
Google Play Store Disclosure
WakaSafe collects location data to enable emergency contact notifications during active journeys, even when the app is closed or not in use. This background location access is required for the application's primary safety function and is disclosed to Google Play in accordance with their background location policy.

Background location is ONLY active when:

  • ✅A journey has been explicitly started by you using your start PIN
  • ✅The SMS interval timer is actively running and scheduled updates are pending
  • ✅An SOS emergency alert has been triggered

Background location is NEVER used for:

  • 🚫Advertising, targeting, or user profiling
  • 🚫Passive monitoring when no journey is active
  • 🚫Sharing your location with Voddic LTD without your direct action
  • 🚫Any commercial purpose beyond delivering safety notifications

Background location access automatically ceases when a journey is ended (via end PIN or distress PIN) or if the application is manually closed during a non-active state.

Contact Permission Usage
O WakaSafe fo no has access to your device address book

Access to your device's contacts is entirely optional. WakaSafe used contacts picker and not READ_CONTACTS permission, which is a convenience feature — to allow you to select emergency contacts directly from your address book rather than typing phone numbers manually.

  • 📖Contact picker is only used to display a selectable list of contacts within the emergency contact setup screen
  • 🔒Voddic does not upload, sync, or store your full contact list on our servers
  • 💾Only the name and number of contacts you explicitly select and save as emergency contacts are retained on your local phone and not on our server
  • 🚫Your contacts are never shared with, sold to, or accessed by advertisers or any third party
  • ❌You can deny this permission and manually enter emergency contact numbers instead — full app functionality is retained

Subscription & Payment Information
How we handle billing and financial data

WakaSafe operates a subscription model to sustain and improve the service. All payment transactions are processed through Paystack, a PCI-DSS compliant payment processing platform widely used across Africa.

  • 🔐WakaSafe does not store, access, or process your payment card details directly
  • 🧾Transaction reference numbers and subscription status records are maintained to verify your active subscription
  • 🏦All payment data is governed by Paystack's Privacy Policy — we encourage you to review it at paystack.com
  • 📅Subscription expiry dates are stored to enforce access controls within the app
ℹ️
What We Keep
We only retain a record that payment was made, when it expires, and the transaction reference from Paystack. No card numbers, CVVs, or banking details are ever stored on WakaSafe's servers.
