Description of Service

WakaSafe is an offline-first Android application that enables travelers to broadcast their real-time GPS location to designated emergency contacts via native SMS messages — without requiring an active internet connection.

The core features of WakaSafe include, but are not limited to:

  • Journey initiation by specifying origin, destination, vehicle type, plate number, and SMS update interval
  • Automated periodic SMS transmission of GPS coordinates and journey details to emergency contacts
  • A 4-digit PIN system to start and end journeys securely
  • A secret distress PIN that silently triggers an emergency SOS SMS to all registered contacts
  • Journey history log with broadcast status records
  • Subscription-based access enforced via a secured payment gateway

Voddic reserves the right to modify, suspend, or discontinue any feature of the service at any time with or without notice.

07

PIN Security System

WakaSafe employs a 4-digits PIN security framework. Each PIN serves a distinct purpose:

  • Start PIN: A 4-digit PIN used to activate and begin a journey. Upon correct entry, a journey start SMS is sent to all emergency contacts.
  • End PIN: A 4-digit PIN used to safely conclude a journey. Upon correct entry, a "arrived safely" SMS with final location and journey duration is sent to all contacts.
  • Distress PIN: A separate 4-digit PIN that externally mimics an end journey action but internally triggers a silent SOS emergency alert to all registered contacts. This PIN is designed for use under duress or threat.
Critical Warning: The distress PIN is a life-safety feature. Deliberately misusing the distress PIN for non-emergency purposes, including testing, is strongly discouraged and may cause unnecessary alarm to your emergency contacts. Voddic accepts no liability for any harm, distress, or consequences arising from accidental or intentional misuse of the distress PIN.

You are solely responsible for setting, remembering, and safeguarding all your PINs. Voddic does not store your PINs in recoverable form. Lost PINs may require account-level intervention through customer support.
