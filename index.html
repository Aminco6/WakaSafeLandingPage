How It Works

Simple. Powerful.
Life-saving.

From the moment you board, WakaSafe silently works — sending your GPS coordinates to loved ones so they always know where you are, no matter the network, No Internet required.

01
📝
Fill Journey Details
Enter your origin, destination, vehicle type, plate number, and how often you want your location sent — every 5, 10, 15, or 30 minutes.
02
🔐
Activate with Your PIN
Enter your personal 4-digit start PIN to activate the journey. A journey start SMS is immediately sent to all your added and trusted emergency contacts.
03
📍
Automatic SMS Updates
At every interval, the app sends a live GPS link, ETA, elapsed time, vehicle details and timestamp to your trusted contacts — all via native SMS.
04
✅
End Journey Safely
On arrival, enter your end PIN to safely close the journey. A final "arrived safely" SMS with exact location is sent to all trusted contacts automatically.

PIN Security

Three PINs.
Three lifelines.

WakaSafe uses a three-PIN security system designed for Nigerian road realities — covert protection when you need it most.

🚀
Start PIN
Activates the journey and sends your first location broadcast to all emergency contacts. Choose any 4-digit PIN you'll remember.
🏁
End PIN
Gracefully closes the journey and sends a final "arrived safely" SMS with your exact location and total journey duration.
⚠️
Distress PIN
A different secret PIN used under threat. Works like an end PIN but fires a silent SOS emergency SMS to all trusted added contacts — no indication on screen that anything different happened.

Features

Everything you need.
Nothing you don't.

📡
100% Offline SMS
No internet or data needed to send location updates. Works on 2G, 3G, or any SMS-capable network across Nigeria.
📍
Live GPS Location
Every SMS contains a live Google Maps link with exact latitude/longitude so your contacts can see precisely where you are.
👥
Multiple Emergency Contacts
Add as many family members or trusted friends as you want. Every SMS update goes to all of them simultaneously.
⏱
Custom SMS Intervals
Choose how often your location is broadcast — every 5, 10, 15, 30 or 60 minutes. Match your comfort to your journey length.
🆘
Silent SOS / Distress PIN
Under threat? Enter your secret distress PIN. It looks like a normal end journey but silently fires a full emergency alert to all contacts.
📜
Journey History
All past journeys are saved with route, vehicle, duration, and all message logs — so you have a full travel record.
🌙
Dark Mode UI
Designed for night travel with a deep dark interface that's easy on the eyes whether it's 6 AM or midnight.
🔒
Subscription Gated
Affordable subscription plans via Paystack keep the service running. Active subscription verified before each journey.
