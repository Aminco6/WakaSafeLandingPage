Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about WakaSafe

How does WakaSafe work without internet? ▼
WakaSafe uses native SMS to send your live location. Once set up, it works completely offline on any network (MTN, Airtel, Glo, 9mobile).
How do I activate a journey? ▼
1. Fill your journey details
2. Enter your 4-digit Start PIN
3. The app will automatically start sending SMS updates to your emergency contacts.
What are the three PINs? ▼
Start PIN: Activates the journey
End PIN: Safely ends the journey and sends final SMS
Distress PIN: Silent SOS — sends emergency alert without showing anything unusual on screen.
How do I turn on SMS Settings? ▼
Go to Settings → SMS Settings → Enable SMS Alerts and choose your preferred interval (10, 15, 30, or 60 minutes).
Can my family track me in real-time? ▼
Yes. Every SMS contains a Google Maps link with your exact location. Your contacts don’t need the app to view it.
What happens if I lose my phone? ▼
As long as the journey is active and the phone has battery and network, it will continue sending location updates using the last known PIN.
